      Usage

      Click any node to expand its similar artists. Click an expanded node again to collapse it back to a leaf.

      Drag empty space to pan, scroll to zoom. Click the ⌖ button to recenter on the root artist.

      Once a root is set, the amber input traces a chain between the root and a second artist through the similarity network.

      About

      Search any artist to see their similar artists as a force-directed graph. Click any node to expand its neighbors, or trace a chain between two artists through Last.fm's similarity network.

      An unofficial graphical frontend for Last.fm. Not affiliated with or endorsed by Last.fm.
